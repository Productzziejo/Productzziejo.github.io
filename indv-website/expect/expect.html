What to Expect

Getting Ready for the Jump? Awesome. We'll help you get all ready to go.

BEFORE YOU COME

Follow this checklist to make sure you're Fruit Falling ready.

  1. Come Hungry! Don't want you vomiting all over yourself in the sky.
    Trust us. Not pretty.
    Besides, you have so much delicous fruit to look forward to.
  2. Wear something athletic and comfortable with sneakers/sport shoes.
    Things like sandals, boots or high heels might look nice, but don't make for an epic skydiving expirience.
  3. Make sure to bring a valid photo ID.
  4. Bring your form of payment! Cash, Checks, Cards, as long as you bring it, we're cool.
  5. We've got Wifi for your wait time, so bring your phones. You can lock them up before you fall. No phone falling here.
  6. Plan to spend several hours here! Time varies.

WHEN YOU ARRIVE

We're here for you guys. Here's exactly what to expect when you get here.

  1. Go ahead and park anywhere in the lot. Come on in!
  2. Check in with one of our sweet employees at the front desk.
  3. Go through your waiver. Legal stuff.
  4. Pay at the same front desk, then just wait there in the lobby (enjoying the free wifi) until we come get you!
  5. You'll be guided throught the rest! You'll lock up your belongings, get suited up, instructed, and head out to dive!
  6. Keep in mind that we respect the power of weather here at fruit falling. It could delay your jump, so read up before you come!
