Worried About Weather?
No Worries.

Here's how it works.

Skydiving is a weather sensitive art. Our ground control team will begin assessing the weather when operations begin for that day. The weather will be constantly monitored throughout the day.

You should make sure to plan to spend a number of hours for your experience. If the conditions are not suitable, your experience may take a little longer.

While at the drop zone, be sure to pay attention to the announcements.

If we go on stand-by, this means that we have to put a hold to our skydiving operations. We will keep you updated whether we will stay on stand-by or cancel for the day.

If you are unable to skydive due to bad weather conditions, your deposit is totally refundable. You can also ask at manifest to move your reservation to a later date if there is space available in our schedule.

We'll all do our best to make it easy and awesome!
