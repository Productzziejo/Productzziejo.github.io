Fruit Friends

What are Fruit Friends?

Skydiving clearlyis more fun than anything else to do,
but we will say this, sometimes it can feel a little lonely.
Except your instructor. They're cool but you just met!
You're all alone. Or are you?
If you are one to get lonely easily, we have your solution!
Fruit Friends is the added option of creating a fruit friend,
face and all, to dive with you. Or die next to you. Your call.

DIVE WITH FRIENDS



How it Works

You jump out of the plane and get settled in your dive, but don't worry, you're not alone. You're falling next to the special friend you've created and taken time to get to know on the ground. You fall together for a while and then deploy your chutes together. Then at the last moment, you can take your fruit friend home with you. Share those memories together. Or drop Fruit Friends from their ‘chutes and watch their ultimate death. We're fine either way.

Why it’s Awesome

Something is just more special about falling with friends, there at your side at all moments.
