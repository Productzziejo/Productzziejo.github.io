Sweet Samurai Skydives

Do You Have the Courage?

If falling out of the sky,
swirling towards the ground at great speeds
isn’t a big enough thrill, you’ve come to the right place. Not everyone has the courage to skydive,
but only the very elite have enough inner strength to take on our “Sweet Samurai Skydive".

How it Works

In this new and exciting thrill of epic proportions, you will get to fulfill your dreams of becoming a literal fruit ninja, while falling from the sky. As part of our most intricate falling experience, you will dive along with 5 expert divers. These professional skydivers, dressed in their samurai best, will fill the position of your fruit- foes, who throw fruit at you with all their might. Your task? Protect yourself and your diving instructor (consider him/her your damsel in destress) from these delicious attacks. Slice, Dice, destroy, mince if you want, with your provided samurai sword. After the fruit-foes retreat, defeated, you will decent victories, upon the remains of these fruit attacks.
