Where Will You Dive?

ALRIGHT! Let'g get your ultimate fruit falling location.

Now, be careful, this is crucial.
This choice will effect the look of your view, availability of local fruits, and availability to falling worthy weather.
Here we go...



Hawaii

Absolutely gorgeous tropic views full of rainforests, oceans and beaches!
Warm, thick air surrounds you in this fall.
More importantly, all of our tropic fruit options are locally grown and perfectly ripe!
Hawaii has seasons, but we don't really know what they are. Dive any time of the year at this location.


Washington

Grand views of the evergreen state that include endless trees, mountains and serenity. The clean, crisp air will make you feel alive in the northwest.
Love apples? Washington has the largest selection of local, fresh apples that will make your mouth water. Also enjoy locally grown berries. (All tropical fruit options are imported.)
Dive in Western Washington’s best weather, between June 15th and September 10th


Utah

Utah’s vast views will wow you with clear skies, surrounding mountains and a lot of room for diving, as well as fruit destruction!
Not much is grown here (unless you want a potato — sorry, that was uncalled for. We don’t provide potatoes), but that just gives us more room to spread out and fall!
Wanna dive in a snowstorm? Didn’t think so. Enjoy Utah between April 15th and October 1st.
