What is Our Company All About?

Fruit Falling, one of the many leading
organizations from Productzziejo,
prides itself on providing a absolutely unique experience that can be found nowhere else.
We can proudly claim that no other company in this civilized world
provides the thrill of skydiving with fruit,
not even to mention our other more unique options.


How did “Fruit Falling” come to be?

One day a young entrepreneur sat with a fellow student,
stumped by lack of new ideas, and lack of working internet connection.
This young person explained to a friend that they needed a new, hip idea, any idea, to give to the people.
It could be anything from skydiving to bananas.
“Skydiving with bananas?”
And thus “fruit falling” was born.
